Team Time Activity Tracker

Simple internal tool for logging and viewing daily time entries.

What is this?

This lightweight app lets anyone on the team quickly log what they worked on and see everyone's entries for the day. There is no login or roles just a shared view of activity.

Log time

Capture a new time entry with date, person, team, activity, and duration.

View entries

Browse and filter all logged entries by date and person.
